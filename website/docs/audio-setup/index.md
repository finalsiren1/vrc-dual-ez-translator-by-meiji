# 🎧 Audio Setup & Routing

Welcome to the Audio Setup guide. Properly routing your audio is the most critical step to ensure SyncVRC can "hear" you and other players clearly. 

In this section, we will cover how to set up both your physical microphone for Outgoing translations and a Virtual Audio device for Incoming translations.

Please select a specific guide from the sidebar on the left, or click **"Next"** to begin setting up your Outgoing Microphone.
