# Settings & Configuration

Welcome to the Settings guide. The **Settings** tab allows you to customize SyncVRC's behavior, appearance, and core processing engines to perfectly suit your preferences.

In this section, we will cover:
* **General Settings:** UI Language, Dark Mode, and Telemetry options.
* **API & Translation Engine:** Choosing your AI provider (e.g., Gemini, DeepL, OpenAI), managing API keys, and selecting your AI Processing Device (GPU vs. CPU).
* **Shortcut Keys:** Configuring custom hotkeys for Outgoing, Incoming, and Push-to-Talk modes.

Please select a specific guide from the sidebar on the left, or click **"Next"** to begin configuring your General Settings.
